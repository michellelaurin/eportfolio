Outcome 5D: Demonstrate effective professional communication to achieve common understanding.

Public Schools and Reluctant Readers Poster Presentation for LIS 701: Core Values, Ethics and Issues in Library and Information Profession.

For this outcome, I created an online poster board presentation on public schools and reluctant readers. I made the poster board for a professional presentation for other librarians as a part of my 701 coursework. The information provided on the poster board was to highlight the main points of my talk, focusing on the background, literature review, the core values of the school library, and concluding remarks. Through this, the goal was to achieve a common understanding among school librarian professionals in alternative practices and methods they can implement in their libraries and with their teachers to achieve reading success with their reluctant readers.

Outcome 5E: Demonstrate leadership principles in an educational, professional, or community context.

Leadership Paper for LIS 707: Leadership, Marketing, and Strategic Communication.

As a part of my 707 coursework, I chose to focus on the leadership theory provided by Brene Brown's book Dare To Lead and her work on leadership and vulnerability. In this paper, I interviewed two children librarians within the Chicago Public Library system to find out their leadership style and the leadership environment of their superiors. The leadership principles found through this paper illustrate how strong leadership from a vulnerability theory viewpoint does not mean having the title of leader but how one implements the principles in their professional workspace. This artifact notes how these leadership principles impact the library settings of the two interviewees, which in turn play a role in how patrons and superiors view them in a professional and community context.
